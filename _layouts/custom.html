Projects

DanNet

DanNet is a networking library that I created during my time at AIE. It's built upon Winsock2 and only uses UDP sockets. It features reliable UDP packets and simulated lag.

Check out the GitHub page at: DanNetGitHub

Codename: Pariah

I am currently apart of a team of 11 people working on Codename: Pariah for our end of year project. The game is a fast paced FPS inspired by the Wolfenstein games and Doom. My work has been primarily creating the FPS controller and ground combat mechanics. A download link will be available soon.
